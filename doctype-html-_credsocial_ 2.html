Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is CRED Social in simple terms?
It's like "karma" on Reddit, but for the entire Web3 ecosystem and with real value. You earn points for good behavior and can use them to unlock bonuses.
Is it safe? Will my data be stolen?
We do not store private keys or collect unnecessary data. Your entire reputation score is calculated based on public on-chain data and verified by our AI.
How do you prevent bots and Sybil attacks?
We combine AI-based detection with on-chain heuristics and anomaly monitoring to reduce manipulation (bot activity, fake engagement, coordinated wallets). Suspicious patterns can be penalized.
What data do you use to calculate reputation?
Only signals that can be verified: public on-chain activity (wallet age, transactions, governance participation) plus selected social signals (community engagement, referrals). No private keys or seed phrases.
Where can I ask a question about the project?
You can ask us in our community on Twitter(X) or Telegram, all the links are at the bottom of the page.
